Thanks for participating in this HIT! You will read a sentence fragment describing a belief someone reading a news headline would have. This belief either describes the intent of an author writing the headline, how a reader would react to that headline, or actions a reader would take after reading about the headline. Please rate the quality of the belief description based on the following questions:

  1. Does the belief description impact whether or not you think the headline is trustworthy?
  2. What is the overall quality of the belief description in terms of coherence and relevance?
  3. Does the belief convey feelings or thoughts that are (generally) considered socially acceptable?
  4. Do you think it is possible that the belief and/or news event perpetuate negative social biases or stereotypes?
