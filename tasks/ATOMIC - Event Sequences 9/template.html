Overview

Thanks for participating in this HIT!

For this task, you will read a short sentence fragment depicting an event. The names of specific people have been replaced by generic words (e.g. "PersonX", "PersonY").

Your goal is to create a short story using event phrases.

  1. Read the given event.
  2. Describe what PersonX did before the event.
    • Ask yourself:
      • Is there a specfic action they needed to take before?
      • What would most people do before the event?
  3. Describe what PersonX will do after the event.
    • Ask yourself:
      • Is there a specific action they need to take after?
      • What would most people do after the event?
  4. Mark "Event makes no sense" if you are unable to create a story.
    • NOTE: We have prefiltered the events so that most of them should make sense.
Guidelines
  • Use present tense.
  • Use actions.
  • Be specific (see examples).
  • Do not use wants, feelings, emotions (see examples).
  • Do not use passive statements (see examples).
