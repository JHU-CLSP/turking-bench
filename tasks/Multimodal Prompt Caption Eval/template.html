Overview

Thanks for participating in this HIT!

In this HIT, you'll be given an image and three sentences that describe the image based on one of the following styles:

  • caption: describes what is in the image with accurate information.
  • news: a news article / headline about the image.
  • story: a coherent and interesting story that someone would tell about an image.
  • instruction: a set of instructions related to the image content.
  • sns: user comment about the image.
You will be asked to provide a rating from scale of 1 to 5 (5 as the highest) based on the following criteria:
  • Image Relevant: Does the sentence include content relevant to the image?
    • 5: Sentence precisely describes what is shown in the image, or content very related to the image based on the given style.
    • 4: Choose this score when you are hesitant between the score 3 and score 5.
    • 3: Sentence displays minor inconsistency with the image, or contains content partially related to the image.
    • 2: Choose this score when you are hesitant between the score 1 and score 3.
    • 1: Sentence contains contradicted information and content not related to the image at all.

  • Informative/Interesting: Does the sentence provide informative and interesting content specific to the image?
    • 5: Sentence is highly informative and has interesting content based on the given style. In addition, it displays a high level of diversity.
    • 4: Choose this score when you are hesitant between the score 3 and score 5.
    • 3: Sentence contains some interesting information about the image, but may be repetitive.
    • 2: Choose this score when you are hesitant between the score 1 and score 3.
    • 1: Sentence is dull, and does not have any interesting information about the image.

  • Fluent / Human-Like: Does the sentence sound fluent and human-like? Is there any contradictory or incoherent content within the sentence?

    • Please read the NOTE below.

    • 5: Sentence is grammatically correct, matches the given style appropriately, and is human-like. This is what I expect humans would say about the image.
    • 4: Choose this score when you are hesitant between the score 3 and score 5.
    • 3: Sentence contains minor grammar errors and may have minor issue with coherency.
    • 2: Choose this score when you are hesitant between the score 1 and score 3.
    • 1: Sentence does not make sense and it is unreadable.
NOTE:
  • Some sentences are forced to truncated to 20 words. Please consider up to non-truncated portion (e.g. previous sentence) when you choose your ratings, especially for fluency.

Pleas provide a rating from scale of 1 to 5 (5 as the highest).
