Is the mapping between two events valid according to the given relation?

Instructions

  • Read the given assertion.
  • Judge if the assertion is valid.
  • To receive credit, please ensure that you have answered all the questions before submitting.

Examples:

Sentence Valid ? Why ?
PersonX would pass -- effect on PersonX -- thrilled about it Passing an exam/class usually makes people happy or excited.
PersonX keeps PersonY happy -- attributes of PersonX -- thoughtful If PersonX cares about PersonY's happiness, PersonX is a thoughtful person.
PersonX goes to the mall with his mom -- intent of PersonX -- his money This phrase does not make sense and hence, is not valid.
PersonX wins the match -- effect on PersonX -- confused People are usually not confused after they win a match.
PersonX breaks a bone -- what others besides PersonX might want to do after the event -- help PersonX People usually help others who are injured or hurt.
